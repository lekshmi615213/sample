What is it?

An online marketplace where businesses can bid on opportunities published by Government Entities & Large Enterprises.

For whom?

Small and medium enterprises looking to take advantage of new opportunities to grow their business.

Why Access to Bids?

Get easy and transparent access to new business opportunities, acquire new customers and grow your network.

Objective of the service:

9/10ths Access to Bids service is a first of a kind online bidding portal launched in the KSA. The portal has been designed and built from the ground up to facilitate procurement transactions between validated Businesses and government as well as large entities in KSA. Businesses will be validated and qualified based on their Ministry of Labor and Social Development (MLSD) records as well as their previous engagements with government and large entities.

What is the service:

The 9/10ths A2B service offers registered businesses a number of features and functions that enables access to opportunities published by government and large entities. The opportunities will be published with all the necessary details as well as the required qualification criteria. The businesses will be able to bid on open opportunities that they qualify for. The system will ensure fairness and transparency through providing the same qualification criteria to all registered businesses.

How to apply?

Businesses access the 9/10ths portal and register to the A2B service by completing a company profile. The information provided undergoes a validation process before they can start bidding on the published opportunities.
