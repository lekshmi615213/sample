What is it?

An E-commerce service to help Home Based Businesses with marketing, payments and logitics.

For whom?

Entrepreneurs who want to grow and develop their social media based bussiness.

Why Access to Markets?

A quick and secure way to run a business, expand nationwide without the infrastructure costs and benefit from an end-to-end service, including storage and delivery.

Objective of the service:

The primary objective of the Access to Markets service is to support the growth of both emerging and established Home Based Businesses within the Kingdom by providing them with the needed services to thrive in the E-commerce industry.

What is the service about?

Access to Markets has been created to support growth of businesses through enabling them to create a business relation with their customers based on trust. In order to achieve this, Access to Markets service offers registered businesses a number of features and functions that enables access to a wide customer base, set-up and brand their businesses as they like and provide a secure payment gateway and delivery services to their customers.

How is the service done?

Access to Markets has been created to support growth of businesses through enabling them to create a business relation with their customers based on trust. In order to achieve this, Access to Markets service offers registered businesses a number of features and functions that enables access to a wide customer base, set-up and brand their businesses as they like and provide a secure payment gateway and delivery services to their customers.

Entrepreneurs can establish their internet presence and select from a range of brandings and designs for their store. They can also connect their social media channels to the website, and monitor activity from the same site. This is a simple click and connect process in which the storefront can be quickly setup for trading.

9/10ths is diligently working on providing entrepreneurs struggling with large volumes of stock the option to opt for the storage facility service, which is a secure warehouse location professionally managed by 9/10ths’ partners. Most functions in the warehouse can be automated or outsourced for ease-of-use.

Once a deal has been agreed on a given social media platform, entrepreneurs can login to their 9/10ths Payment Gateway and create an invoice for a customer. The customer can then make an online payment and receive confirmation of a verified transaction.

9/10ths has partnered with a dependable logistics provider to ensure reliable delivery and extensive reach across the KSA. Entrepreneurs utilizing the Access to Markets service will be able to arrange a ‘Collect & Deliver’ option to their customers across the Kingdom.

Businesses will be able to monitor the deliveries to customers, customer requests and communications, and review outstanding invoices. The service will arm the entrepreneur with the information needed to oversee business operations and plan the next steps.

How to apply?

Businesses access the 9/10ths portal and register to the Access to Markets service by completing a company profile. The information provided undergoes a validation process before they can start using the multitude of Access to Markets services.
